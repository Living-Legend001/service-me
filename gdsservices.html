What Is A 5 Page Website?

A 5 page website is a website without a storage or login system, typically consisting of five core pages,
designed for simple information sharing or basic online presence.

What Are The Pages Of A 5 Page Website?

Home Page

This is the main page of the website and should communicate what your website or business is all about, while linking to other important pages of the website.

About Page

This page gives an overview of the individual, company, or service that is represented on the website, and usually includes picures for visual representation.

Product Or Service Page

This page is used to communicate the products or services being sold or provided. A very important page because it is a major decision point for the visitor where they will decide if the product or service being offered is a good fit for them.

Pictures Page

This also is a very important page as some clients may need a Pictures Page to organize and display pictures representing their work.

Testimonials Or References Page

This is a great way to increase confidence in your product or service being offered. It usually includes testimonials or references from previous customers and clients.

Frequently Asked Questions Page

This page can be useful to answer the most common questions visitors have proactively.

A Contact Page

This page is critical to let peope know how they can get in contact with you
